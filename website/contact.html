About

The Data Publication & Consumption Tools Library is provided by the LATC project.

LATC, Linked Open Data Around-The-Clock is a Specific Support Action currently under negotiation in the context of the FP7 ICT Programme. The LATC mission is to support people and organisations to better publish and consume Linked Open Data.
